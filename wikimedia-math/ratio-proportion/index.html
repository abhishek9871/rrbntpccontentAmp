Ratio Definition

Ratio in mathematics shows how many times one number contains another. For example, if there are eight oranges and six lemons in a bowl of fruit, the ratio of oranges to lemons is 8:6 (or 4:3 when simplified).

Types of Ratios

  • Ratio (general): Shows how many times one number contains another. Example: 8 oranges, 6 lemons, ratio of oranges to lemons is 8:6 (or 4:3). The numbers can be quantities of any kind (counts, measurements). In most contexts, both numbers are restricted to be positive.
  • Ratio (physical sciences/metrology): The dimensionless quotient between two physical quantities measured with the same unit.
  • Proportion: A statement expressing the equality of two ratios.
  • Rate: A quotient of two quantities that are measured with different units.

Terms and Notation

  • Terms of the ratio: The numbers A and B in A:B, where A is the antecedent and B is the consequent.
  • Terms of the proportion: A, B, C, D in a proportion A:B = C:D. A and D are called extremes, B and C are called means.
  • Continued proportion: The equality of three or more ratios, e.g., A:B = C:D = E:F.

Notation Examples

  • A to B
  • A:B
  • A is to B (when followed by 'as C is to D')
  • A fraction with A as numerator and B as denominator (A/B)

Proportion Properties

A proportion is a mathematical statement expressing equality of two ratios. It can be written as A:B=C:D or A/B=C/D.

Fundamental Properties of Proportions:

  • Fundamental rule of proportion (Means-Extremes Property / Cross-Products Property): If A/B=C/D, then AD=BC
  • Inverse Property: If A/B=C/D, then B/A=D/C
  • Alternando Property: If A/B=C/D, then A/C=B/D and D/B=C/A
  • Componendo and Dividendo Properties: If A/B=C/D, then (A+B)/B=(C+D)/D and (A-B)/B=(C-D)/D
  • Property of Sum/Difference: If A/B=C/D, then (A+C)/(B+D)=A/B=C/D and (A-C)/(B-D)=A/B=C/D

Mathematical Formulas

Fraction Representation

A/B represents the ratio of A to B

Multiple-term Ratios

For example, dimensional lumber: thickness : width : length = 2:4:10

Concrete mix: cement : sand : gravel = 1:2:4

Percentage from Ratios

To convert ratio to percentage, divide by total sum of parts and multiply by 100:

For substances A,B,C,D in ratio 5:9:4:2 (total 20 parts):

  • A = 5/20 = 25%
  • B = 9/20 = 45%
  • C = 4/20 = 20%
  • D = 2/20 = 10%
